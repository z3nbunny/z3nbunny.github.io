1. Introduction

Welcome to McGovern.Design. I am committed to respecting the privacy of my site visitors. This policy outlines how I handle any information when you visit my portfolio or play the browser-based games hosted here.


2. Information Collection

I do not collect personal data. This website is a static portfolio designed to showcase my work. There are no user accounts, login systems, or hidden tracking pixels installed by me.


3. Local Storage (The Game)

To improve your experience with the "Pink Quadruped" game demo, this site may use your browser's Local Storage. This is used solely to save your:

  • Game high scores
  • Audio settings (Volume On/Off)
  • Unlocked levels

This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any external server. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser cache.


4. External Links

This portfolio contains links to external sites (e.g., GitHub, LinkedIn). I am not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external platforms.
